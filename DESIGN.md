# Design Brief: Shree Real Estate Services

## Overview
High-conversion B2B industrial real estate platform for VKI, Jaipur. Targets business operators, factory owners, investors. Brutalist corporate aesthetic: grid-based, information-dense, minimal ornament. NO luxury residential feel.

## Visual Direction
**Tone**: Utilitarian corporate, grounded, professional, serious. Fast info + clarity + trust.
**Feel**: Brutalist + accessible. Structured hierarchy. No emotional storytelling.
**Differentiation**: Deep green industrial authority + maroon urgency CTAs + monospace property data.

## Color Palette (OKLCH)
| Token | Light | Dark | Purpose |
|-------|-------|------|---------|
| Primary | L32 C0.18 H160 | L65 C0.15 H160 | Industrial trust, buttons |
| Secondary | L25 C0.20 H25 | L60 C0.18 H25 | Urgency, call-to-action |
| Accent | L60 C0.18 H45 | L70 C0.16 H45 | Highlights, visual punctuation |
| Background | L98 C0 H0 | L14 C0 H0 | Page surface |
| Foreground | L12 C0 H0 | L95 C0 H0 | Text |
| Muted | L90 C0 H0 | L25 C0 H0 | Secondary text, borders |

## Typography
- **Display**: Bricolage Grotesque (bold, geometric, distinctive hierarchy)
- **Body**: DM Sans (humanist sans, professional readability)
- **Mono**: Geist Mono (property data, prices, structured info)

## Structural Zones
| Zone | Treatment | Purpose |
|------|-----------|---------|
| Header/Nav | Deep green bg, white text, sticky, minimal padding | Brand identity, fast navigation |
| Hero | Full-width industrial image overlay, deep green CTA | Immediate credibility |
| Property Grid | Light grey background, minimal card borders, no shadows | Information clarity, B2B focus |
| CTA Sections | Maroon/red secondary buttons, high contrast | Urgency, inquiry conversion |
| Footer | Dark grey background, white text, compact | Navigation, contact info |

## Spacing & Rhythm
Grid: 8px/16px/24px/32px. Minimal padding within cards. No excessive whitespace. Information density prioritized.

## Shape Language
Radii: 0 (buttons), 4px (cards), no rounded corners except buttons. Structured, industrial feel.

## Component Patterns
- **Buttons**: Primary (deep green) + Secondary (maroon), no shadows, minimal radii
- **Cards**: Light background, minimal border (1px), no elevation, data-focused
- **Forms**: Clear labels, structured inputs, high contrast

## Motion
Minimal. Smooth transitions (0.3s cubic-bezier) on interactive states only. No entrance animations. No decorative motion.

## Constraints
- Mobile-first responsive
- Fast loading (minimal animation, no heavy effects)
- High contrast for accessibility
- NO gradients, NO luxury effects
- NO residential or emotional design

## Signature Detail
Deep green + maroon + monospace typography = industrial B2B authority. Grid-based structure signals seriousness and professionalism.
